About the program

Incident Review Board

A structured, fact-focused meeting that occurs within 24 hours of any safety incident. It brings together the Collaborative Leadership Team and the individuals involved to answer: why did this happen, and how do we prevent it from ever happening again?

Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

Rather than assigning blame, the IRB uses the "5 Whys" method — asking why repeatedly until the true underlying cause is identified. Every RCA produces specific corrective actions with an assigned owner and a deadline.

Accountability without blame

Grounded in professionalism and mutual respect. Focus is on facts and data, not emotions — creating a culture where employees feel safe surfacing issues early, before they become serious incidents.

Non-negotiables
★Begins with a Champions of Safety moment
★Must occur within 24 hours — no exceptions
★Focus on facts and data, not emotions or blame
★Every corrective action has an owner and deadline
★Follow the approved IRB template for consistency
★Professionalism and mutual respect are mandatory
★Eliminate distractions — give your full attention
★Ask WHY multiple times to reach the true root cause
